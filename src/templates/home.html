CS:GO Sitting Duck

This website will show you a heatmap to help you understand your movements patterns during the game. The idea is to use the CS:GO game integration to receive data from your position during the match and use that data to plot a heatmap showing spots that you usualy are or if you are rushing too much. Second thing would be the location where you were killed to help you understand if you are dying in open areas without cover.

Build with Python Flask
